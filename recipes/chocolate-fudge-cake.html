Chocolate Fudge Cake

Description

This is a simple chocolate cake recipe that can be made in under an hour. For this, the ingredients in the recipe will make a total of 8 serves. This recipe is a good way to start baking due to its easy nature.

Ingredients

Cake

  • 150ml sunflower oil, plus extra for the tin
  • 175g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 150ml semi-skimmed milk
